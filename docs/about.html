About the Creator

Hi, I’m Alex – a synthwave addict, C64 / Amiga lover, and retro-coding enthusiast. I enjoy building demos, writing music inspired by SID and Paula, and recreating the look & feel of late–80s arcade culture.

This website is an experiment: everything is driven by Markdown content, GitHub Copilot prompts, and TailwindCSS – no heavy frameworks, no backend, just clean static files with neon flair.

When I’m not pushing pixels, I’m usually exploring synthwave tracks, writing about retro hardware, or playing classic games like OutRun for the millionth time.
